Samlanh App — Privacy & Usage Policy

Effective date: October 22, 2025

This Privacy & Usage Policy describes how Samlanh App handles user information. Samlanh App allows users to perform simple work-related activities such as Clock In / Clock Out, Request Leave, Request LUY (money), and Create Customer Visit.

1. Information We Collect

Samlanh App collects only basic information necessary to support its main functions. The data collected includes:

  • Clock In / Clock Out: Date and time when users start and finish their work.
  • Request Leave: Reason for leave and requested date and time.
  • Request LUY: Reason for the request and requested amount.
  • Customer Visit: Customer name and purpose or details of the visit.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used only for internal record-keeping and work management purposes. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Record attendance and working hours.
  • Track and manage leave requests.
  • Manage and record LUY (money) requests.
  • Keep a record of customer visits and their purposes.

3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized users or administrators of your organization. We do not share, sell, or transfer your information to any third party.

4. Data Sharing

Samlanh App does not share personal or usage data with external services. All information remains private within your organization for management purposes only.

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in features or practices. The updated date at the top will show when it was last revised.
